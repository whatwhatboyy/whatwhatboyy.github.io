PS3 Modding Safety

PSN Ban Risk

Modded PS3 consoles risk permanent PSN bans. Never connect to PSN with CFW or HEN installed.

Model Compatibility

CFW only works on specific models. Super Slim and newer Slim models require HEN instead.

Temperature Monitoring

Use webMAN or similar tools to monitor temperatures. Overheating can damage your console.

NAND/NOR Backup

Always create firmware backups before installation. This is essential for recovery.
