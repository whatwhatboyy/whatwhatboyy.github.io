Xbox 360 Modding Safety

Risk of Brick

JTAG/RGH installation requires precise soldering and can permanently damage your console if done incorrectly.

Xbox Live Ban

Modded consoles will be permanently banned from Xbox Live. Never connect a modded console to Xbox Live.

Technical Skills Required

Installation requires advanced soldering skills and proper equipment. Consider professional installation.

NAND Backup Important

Always create and verify NAND backups before modification. This is essential for recovery.
