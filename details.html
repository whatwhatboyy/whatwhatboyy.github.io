Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about gaming cheats and tools

Why do I have to disable my antivirus?

Game cheats use code injection techniques that look similar to malware, so antivirus programs flag them even though they are harmless. This is called a "false positive." You can either temporarily disable real-time protection or add an exclusion folder. Always download only from trusted sources and scan files on VirusTotal if you are unsure.

Are the cheats safe to use?

Modding always carries some risk. We curate and test everything we share to minimize issues, but no cheat is 100% undetectable forever. Game updates and new anti-cheat patches can change detection status at any time. Use cheats responsibly and check our Safety page for best practices before you start.

How is everything free?

We rely on ad revenue to keep the site running and all downloads free. The ads help cover hosting, development time, and the effort it takes to find, test, and update every tool. If you enjoy what we offer, consider supporting us by keeping your adblocker off while browsing.
