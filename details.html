Why do I have to disable my antivirus?

Cheats are often detected by antivirus programs as viruses even though they are not. This is called a "false positive." Cheat software uses injection techniques similar to malware, which triggers security warnings. Always scan files with VirusTotal before running them, and only download from trusted sources.

Are all the cheats safe and undetected?

When you are modding there is always a risk. We try to bring you only the best mods out there and make sure you won't get banned. However, no cheat is 100% undetectable forever. Always use cheats responsibly and check our Safety page for best practices.

How do you earn if all the cheats are free?

We use ads in order to keep the content free. Support us by disabling your adblocker! Your support helps us maintain the website and provide updated, quality content for the community. Thanks!
